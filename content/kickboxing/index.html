Kickboxing

I have competed in WAKO kickboxing (point fighting) for over a decade, balancing elite-level competition with doctoral research. WAKO is the only kickboxing federation recognized by the Global Association of International Sports Federations and the International Olympic Committee. In my career, I have won nine national titles and eight World Cups. I am the current World and European Champion and ranked number one in the world.

Athlete Representation

I serve as the athlete representative (Athletensprecher) of the German kickboxing federation (WAKO Deutschland). I previously served on the executive committee of Athleten Deutschland, an independent organization representing German elite athletes, and the athlete committee of the German Olympic Sports Confederation (DOSB) from 2023 to 2025. This work centers on athlete governance, anti-doping policy, and representation of athlete interests in federation decision-making.
