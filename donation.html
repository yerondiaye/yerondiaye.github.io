Selection Process

The donation will be selected using the following transparent and replicable process:

  1. Study Completion: Data collection will close on Aug 31, 2025.
  2. Random Participant Selection: One participant will be randomly selected from all study completers.
  3. Decision Implementation: The selected participant's allocation decision between the American Immigration Council and NumbersUSA will be implemented exactly as they specified.
  4. Donation Execution: The full $500 will be donated according to the participant's allocation within 7 business days of study completion.
