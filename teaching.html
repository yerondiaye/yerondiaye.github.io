Teaching Philosophy

I believe in combining rigorous economic theory with hands-on application. In my courses, I have taught both theory as well as experimental methods to help students develop both analytical skills and an intuitive understanding of strategic interaction. I encourage students to connect microeconomic reasoning to real-world institutions in health care, auctions, and political decision-making. I teach in both German and English and have experience with diverse, international student groups.

Teaching Award
